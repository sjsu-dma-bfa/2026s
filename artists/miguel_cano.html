About the Artist

Hello! My name is Miguel Cano, I was born in Guanajuato, Mexico but was raised in Los Angeles, California since the age of 6. I have always been fascinated by cartoons and anime from a very young age. I remember drawing all sorts of Pokemon in elementary school and giving them out to my friends and classmates. I would be approached by other students asking me to draw their favorite Pokemon, so I learned about commissions very early on. This passion for drawing never stopped since most of my homework margins were covered in doodles. These endless doodles led me to create my own original characters and a want to tell their story. I took an animation class in high school and that's what started my love for making little animations of my characters. Then I took a story telling class and fell in love with the art of telling a story, what entertains people and what will keep them at their edge of their seats waiting for more. Since then I have storyboarded and animated my characters in many different fun scenarios. Animation is a long and tedious process, but when I press play and the animation works as I intended it to work, there is no greater feeling. That is what really pushes me to keep going. I surprise myself every time I make a new animation and it makes me wonder “What else can I do?” and so I keep going and I keep trying.

Cosmic Eternity Opening

This animation is a big milestone for me! Seeing these characters in color projected onto the walls of a theater makes me believe that my goals are obtainable. Cosmic eternity, the adventure of Bagle and Fortune is all about believing in yourself because that's where true power comes from! As cheesy as it sounds its true because, you gotta get yourself out there and reach for the goals you dream of! No one else can do that for you. I want my work to bring smiles, laughter, and hope to people. Bagle and Fortune are heroes and they will stop at nothing to bring the evil and injustices of the world down! No matter how hard things get they will see it through to the end.
