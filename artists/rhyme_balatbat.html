About the Artist

Rhyme Balatbat is an artist who turns weird and crazy ideas into reality through his work. His main focuses are 2D illustration, 3D modeling, and video editing. He's proficient with Blender, Nomad Sculpt, Clip Studio Paint, and various Adobe programs. He combines video editing with his illustration skills to create fun and fantastical artworks. He draws most of his inspiration from different aspects of his childhood, such as candies, food, and places he's visited. He uses his work to tap into the joyful and whimsical parts of life and uses those experiences and feelings to push away the monotone and harsh aspects of reality.

SweetHeart: First Batch

Life is short, so let's make it sweet! SweetHeart: First Batch is a series of blind boxes that reflect my personal adoration for candy and sweets alike. I've always been fascinated with blind boxes, so I decided to create my own. This series features my original character, Ube, sporting four unique candy-clad looks that personify my colourful and whimsical approach to storytelling and design work. The thrill and mystery of a blind box, combined with the joy and whimsy of a fun character design, creates a stir of emotions within me that I hope to recreate in my audience. I want to give others a look into my wacky and colourful mindscape through my art.
