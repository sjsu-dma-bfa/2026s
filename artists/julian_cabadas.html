About the Artist

Born in Santa Cruz as a Mexican-American, Julian Cabadas is a 3D modeler/animator and graphic designer for marketing. Through the course of his life going through college, university and work spaces, he found his appreciation and love for art when he was going through multiple projects both assigned and personal.

Art made him truly feel like he had something he can lean onto when he felt like he needed to tell a story. Julian's practice in 3D modeling started back in 2022 when he first discovered Blender and all the wonders it can do, leading into frame animations that can create stories that he desires to tell. Getting very technical when creating frame shots and artistic style that he wants to incorporate into his worlds. Julian's work adapts to today's workflow on creating stories, but he feels like he takes a lot of inspiration from his past work and hobbies that he used to love and adapts them into something he can feel proud of.

In Your Head

Having that feeling of the unknown, not knowing how the future is going to look, or if the path you are taking is not the right one. In Your Head tells that story with moments that can feel familiar. Someone who keeps striving to keep pushing forward but feels like they don't believe in themselves in the process, and goes through the emotions of anxiety and imposter syndrome. Jayce is our student here, always caring and worried about what's going to happen next, not ready for the unknown yet. He wants to succeed and be proud about his accomplishments he has done so far, but there's always that “what if” thought in his head. Always asking questions to himself like, “How am I here?” “Am I going to get through the semester?” “How am I going to finish all of my work?” Follow Jayce's Journey, there should always be light at the end of every tunnel.
