About the Artist

Alex Rodriguez is a Digital Video Artist born and raised in Dublin, California. His work is primarily focused on sketch based comedy for the sake of making his audience laugh. He is experienced with filmmaking and storytelling in Adobe Premier Pro and After Effects, as well as 3D software such as Blender, and aims to bring a little more elation into the world through the works he makes. He also just likes making things he thinks are cool.

My Silly Little Dungeon

Hey there, folks. Welcome to My Silly Little Dungeon, a fun little interactive video thingie I came up with while taking a shower. It is a video installation where you (yes you!) can press a button! Don't you love pressing buttons? A randomly selected video clip of me (yes me!) exploring a 3D dungeon created in Blender will play out! I've always prioritized making things that are funny and silly, making people chuckle a little bit has always been the primary goal of my work. Tabletop roleplaying games like Dungeons & Dragons have been a very influential part of my life throughout these past few years, and I realized I have never made a piece with those games as my inspiration. And so here we are. I shot the videos myself in my garage, and animated photographs of said videos using Adobe After Effects to create a more cartoon-style squash and stretch that I am, unfortunately, incapable of doing myself. If I made you do a quick nose exhalation watching and interacting with my piece, I consider it a success. I guarantee there are zero spiders.
