About the Artist

Shanglong Chen is a Digital Artist based in the Bay Area. He specializes in a variety of software and skills. Photoshop is one of his greatest proficiencies along with Figma and After Effects. His works aim for a seamless digital experience for all and wants to pursue his focus on UI/UX design. Shanglong is currently in his last semester of studies and is looking to graduate in the spring 2026 with a BFA in Digital Media Arts.

Becoming

Becoming is a exploration of self improvement. Through a series of emotions represented by shapes and movements, the narrative uses motion to explore the dynamic process for forging your identity in the face of corrosive self-doubt. The piece follows the journey of a singular shape that shifts between new phases of their identity as fear and anxiety slowly takes down their self confidence. Self-doubt appears as pressure of sharp spikes that challenges the character's emotions and control of achieving success. The dark visuals capture the elements of fear and how it paralyzes potential through whispering that goal is unattainable. The animation transitions from moments of destruction for ones mental and physical identity to sequences of reconstructing themselves to become the best version. They break through the void of darkness represented by fear and ultimately reshape their identity to who they want to become. Doubt may attempt to deconstruct who we are but it is our choice to act that ultimately defines us.
