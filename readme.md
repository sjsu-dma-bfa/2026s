Welcome to the BFA DMA Exhibition Show of Spring 2026!
======================================================

* * *

### DESCRIPTION

The **Digital Media Art (DMA) Program of Spring 2026** proudly announces _Re-Mix Division_, the Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) exhibition of graduating students from **San José State University**. As part of the **40th Anniversary of the _CADRE Media Lab_**, we are showcasing bold new works in video, installation, projection mapping, 3D media, video games, painting, and interactive pieces. _Re-Mix Division_ explores how contemporary artists remix technology, identity, and digital culture. Through experimental approaches and hybrid media, the artists challenge traditional boundaries between the physical and digital world, inviting viewers to reflect on how technology shapes creativity, perception, and everyday life.

Join us on **April 18th, 2026**, from 7-9 pm with a 6-7 pm reception at the **SJSU Hammer Theatre Center** in **Downtown San José** for an immersive show of innovation and experimentation. The exhibition brings together a diverse group of emerging artists whose works engage with themes of interactivity, networks, virtual environments, and digital storytelling. _Re-Mix Division_ celebrates the culmination of their studies and highlights the future of digital media art as it continues to evolve within contemporary culture.
