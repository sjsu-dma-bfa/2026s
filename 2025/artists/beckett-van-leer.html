About the Artist

Beckett Van Leer is a character designer and illustrator born in Santa Clara and currently based in the Bay Area. With their time at SJSU, they have expanded their skills in traditional mediums while also learning about and transitioning into digital technologies such as 3D sculpting, printing, animation; digital video; and digital illustration. Van Leer has taken their love of sculpting with clay and applying it to digital 3D mediums. Their aim with 3D printing is to create an interactive product that merges visual aesthetics with curiosity and excitement.
