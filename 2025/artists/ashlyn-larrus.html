About the Artist

Ashlyn Larrus is a digital artist with a passion for storytelling and world building. She enjoys illustrating characters, environments, and fan-works of her favorite games.

Everyday

Everyday is a short animation about a boy battling his habitual substance abuse, originally introduced to him by the environment he grew up in. As he ages, his compulsions worsen until it eventually begins to impact the relationships around him.
