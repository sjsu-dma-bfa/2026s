About the Show

The Digital Media Art (DMA) Program of Spring 2026 proudly announces Re-Mix Division, the Bachelor Fine Arts (BFA) exhibition of graduating students from San José State University's. As part of the 40th Anniversary of the CADRE Media Lab, we are showcasing bold new works in video, installation, projection mapping, 3D media, video games, painting, and interactive pieces. Re-Mix Division explores how contemporary artists remix technology, identity, and digital culture. Through experimental approaches and hybrid media, the artists challenge traditional boundaries between the physical and digital world, inviting viewers to reflect on how technology shapes creativity, perception, and everyday life.

Details

Join us on April 18th, 2026 from 7-9pm with a 6-7pm reception at the SJSU Hammer Theatre Center in Downtown San José for an immersive show of innovation and experimentation. The exhibition brings together a diverse group of emerging artists whose works engage with themes of interactivity, networks, virtual environments, and digital storytelling. Re-Mix Division celebrates the culmination of their studies and highlights the future of digital media art as it continues to evolve within contemporary culture.
